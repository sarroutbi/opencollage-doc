\section{Core Architecture}

\begin{frame}{Architecture: Four Pillars}
  \begin{block}{Core Message}
    \scriptsize
    OpenCollage is built on four pillars: \textbf{Pipeline Definitions},
    \textbf{State Management}, \textbf{Observability}, and
    \textbf{Standardized Communication}---each designed as an open, extensible standard.
  \end{block}
